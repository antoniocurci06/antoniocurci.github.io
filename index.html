Curriculum Vitae

PhD Student in Artificial Intelligence for Society at the University of Bari in co-affiliation with the University of Pisa. He is part of the Information Visualization and Usability (IVU) Laboratory and his supervisor is Professor Rosa Lanzilotti, who is a Full Professor at the University of Bari. His main interests are Human-Computer Interaction and its relationship with Artificial Intelligence, along with with the ramifications in Project Management, Software Engineering, and Cybersecurity. He is a member of the Italian Research Staff Association (ItalianRSA) since November 2022, the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) since December 2023, and the SIGCHI Italy (Italian Chapter of the Special Interest Group on Computer-Human Interaction) since April 2023, ACM SIGCHI (ACM Special Interest Group on Computer-Human Interaction) since May 2023,
